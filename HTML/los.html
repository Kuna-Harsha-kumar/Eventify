WHY WATCH LOS ANGELES OPERA - OTELLO?

The opera is known for its powerful score, which features sweeping melodies, lush orchestration, and dramatic choral passages. The vocal parts are demanding, particularly for the lead roles of Otello and Iago, and require singers with a strong dramatic presence. The production of Otello by Los Angeles Opera is known for its impressive staging and design, which brings the story to life with rich and vivid imagery. The production features a cast of world-class opera singers, as well as a full orchestra and chorus. Los Angeles Opera is one of the premier opera companies in the United States, known for its commitment to producing innovative and engaging productions of classic operas. Otello is just one example of the company's dedication to bringing the best in operatic art to audiences in Los Angeles and beyond.

WHAT'S THE STORY?

Los Angeles Opera's Otello is an operatic adaptation of Shakespeare's classic tragedy, Othello. It was composed by Italian composer Giuseppe Verdi, with a libretto by Arrigo Boito. The opera tells the story of the Moorish general Otello, who is tricked into believing that his wife, Desdemona, has been unfaithful. The deceit is carried out by Otello's trusted advisor, Iago, who is jealous of Otello's relationship with Desdemona.
