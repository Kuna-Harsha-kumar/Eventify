WHY WATCH JURASSIC WORLD LIVE?

ONCE UPON A TIME

Jurassic World Live is a touring arena show that brings the world of Jurassic Park and Jurassic World to life in a thrilling live action performance. The show features animatronic dinosaurs, stunts, and special effects, as well as a cast of live actors and performers. Among the dinosaurs featured in the show are the T-Rex, Velociraptor, Stegosaurus, and Triceratops, all of which are recreated with incredible attention to detail. Jurassic World Live offers an immersive and action-packed experience for fans of the Jurassic Park and Jurassic World franchises, and is a must-see for anyone who loves dinosaurs and thrilling live entertainment.

WHAT'S THE STORY?

The story follows a team of scientists who are working to save the dinosaurs from an imminent volcanic eruption on the island of Isla Nublar, the setting of the Jurassic World films. As the story unfolds, the team of scientists works to transport the dinosaurs off the island and to safety, while facing off against dangerous mercenaries who are looking to capture the dinosaurs for their own nefarious purposes. Along the way, the team also uncovers a plot to create genetically modified dinosaurs, which threatens the balance of nature and the safety of humanity.
