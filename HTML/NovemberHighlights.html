November Highlights

BEETLEJUICE

"Beetlejuice" is a musical based on the 1988 film of the same name. The story follows the recently deceased couple, Barbara and Adam, who are stuck haunting their old house in New England. When a new family moves in, the couple tries to scare them off, but their efforts are thwarted by the mischievous and chaotic ghost, Beetlejuice. The musical features an upbeat and energetic score, spectacular visual effects, and choreography that brings the world of the afterlife to life on stage.

Pantages Theatre Hollywood, From November 11 - 30, 2023

ANTZ

"Antz" is a computer-animated adventure-comedy film released in 1998. The story follows Z, an ant with individualistic tendencies who yearns for a life beyond his mundane existence. He falls in love with Princess Bala and becomes involved in a power struggle against General Mandible. The film addresses themes of individuality, conformity, and social hierarchy, with a star-studded voice cast including Woody Allen, Sharon Stone, and Sylvester Stallone.

Audrey Skirball Kenis Theater, From November 20 - December 30, 2023
