CATS

"Cats" is a musical that follows the story of a group of Jellicle cats, who gather for their annual Jellicle Ball. Throughout the show, each cat introduces themselves and shares their unique story and personality through song and dance. The main focus is on Grizabella, an older cat who is ostracized by the others, and her journey of redemption. The show features elaborate costumes and makeup, as well as memorable songs like "Memory."

Fred Kavli Theatre, From August 11 - 14, 2023

HAIRSPRAY

"Hairspray" is a musical comedy set in 1962 Baltimore that tells the story of Tracy Turnblad, a plus-sized teenager with a big heart and even bigger dreams. Despite being ridiculed by her peers, Tracy is determined to dance on "The Corny Collins Show." With infectious music, upbeat dance numbers, and a heartwarming message, "Hairspray" celebrates diversity, acceptance, and the power of following your dreams.

Dolby Theatre, From August 2 - 21, 2023
