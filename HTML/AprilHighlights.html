RUSSIAN GRAND BALLET: SWAN LAKE

Russian Grand Ballet's production of "Swan Lake" is a stunning showcase of classical ballet at its finest. Featuring world-class dancers and exquisite choreography, this timeless tale of love and betrayal comes to life on stage in a dazzling display of beauty and grace. The lavish sets and Tchaikovsky's timeless score provide the perfect backdrop for this unforgettable story of passion and redemption.

Irvine Barclay Theatre, From April 18 - 19, 2023

SUNDAY IN THE PARK WITH GEORGE

"Sunday in the Park with George" is a powerful musical exploring the relationship between art, creativity, and human connection. It follows George Seurat as he paints his masterpiece while navigating personal struggles and emotional complexity. With stunning visuals and a soaring score, this production is a moving tribute to the sacrifices made in the pursuit of artistic greatness.

Pasadena Playhouse, Until April 19, 2023
