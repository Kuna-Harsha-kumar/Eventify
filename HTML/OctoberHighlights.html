INTO THE WOODS

"Into the Woods" is a musical that intertwines the plots of several classic fairy tales, including Cinderella, Jack and the Beanstalk, Little Red Riding Hood, and Rapunzel. The story follows a baker and his wife who are trying to break a curse placed on their family by a witch. They journey into the woods to find the ingredients needed for the curse-breaking spell and cross paths with other characters from the fairy tales along the way.

Ahmanson Theatre, From September 27 - October 30, 2023

MORGAN WALLEN

Morgan Wallen is a country music singer and songwriter from Tennessee. He gained fame in 2018 with his debut album "If I Know Me," which features hit singles like "Up Down" and "Whiskey Glasses." In 2021, he released his second album "Dangerous: The Double Album," which became a massive commercial success, reaching number one on the Billboard 200 chart.
