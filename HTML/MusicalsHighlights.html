Musicals Highlights

LION KING

The Lion King tells the story of Simba (Swahili for lion), a lion cub who is to succeed his father, Mufasa, as King of the Pride Lands. After Mufasa is killed by his uncle Scar, Simba is tricked into exile. Years later, encouraged by friends and guided by wisdom, Simba returns to reclaim his rightful place in the Circle of Life.

Pantages Theater Hollywood, Until Mar 26, 2023

LES MISÉRABLES

"Les Misérables" is a musical based on Victor Hugo's novel. It follows Jean Valjean, a former prisoner seeking redemption, and his relentless pursuer Javert. With beloved songs like "I Dreamed a Dream" and "Do You Hear the People Sing?", it explores themes of justice, sacrifice, love, and revolution.

Pantages Theatre Hollywood, From August 1 - September 10, 2023

Buy Tickets

SIX

"Six" is a musical retelling the stories of Henry VIII’s six wives through a modern pop concert. Each queen takes the mic to share her story in an energetic, empowering format that’s full of catchy music, witty lyrics, and female solidarity. It’s a fierce and fun feminist take on history.

Pantages Theatre Hollywood, From July 9 - August 10, 2023
